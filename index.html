Who We Are

Founded in 2021, Faranani Shoe Cleaning started as a small passion project focused on providing affordable, reliable, and environmentally conscious shoe care. Today, we’ve become a trusted name in Johannesburg, serving hundreds of customers with pride and precision.

Our mission is simple: to make every shoe feel brand new while promoting sustainability and empowering local youth through skill development in cleaning and restoration.

Our Key Services

Professional Cleaning

From sneakers to leather shoes, we remove stains, dirt, and odor using high-quality, safe cleaning products.

Shoe Restoration

Bring old shoes back to life with our advanced restoration techniques that revive color, texture, and shape.

Polishing & Shine

Get that fresh-from-the-store shine with our detailed polishing service for all shoe materials.

Pickup & Delivery

Enjoy doorstep convenience — we collect, clean, and deliver your shoes right to your home or office.
