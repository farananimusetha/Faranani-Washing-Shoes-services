Our Story

Every great journey begins with a single step — ours started with a single pair of dirty sneakers.

Who We Are

Faranani Shoe Cleaning was founded with one mission — to restore confidence one step at a time. We are a Johannesburg-based shoe care company specializing in professional cleaning, repairs, and restoration. Our name “Faranani” means *“to support each other”*, and that’s exactly what we do — uplift our clients, our community, and our environment.

We aim to empower young people by providing job training in sustainable shoe care and encourage recycling through responsible practices. With over 1,000 pairs cleaned and dozens of satisfied clients, our story continues to grow — step by step.
